Scholarly Focus

Research Interests

Crisis Communication
Community-based approaches to crisis management — examining how communities bond, cope, and construct meaning during disasters and organizational crises.
Environmental CSR
Corporate social responsibility in the environmental domain — how organizations communicate purpose and impact to diverse, sometimes politically divided, stakeholder publics.
Social Identity
Identity-based frameworks for understanding organization-public relationships, including how identity salience and intergroup dynamics shape engagement and perceptions.
Social-Mediated Engagement
Digital public relations, media affordances, and social network analysis — how social platforms shape activism, polarized discourse, and organization-public interactions.
